Member Join Form

Members graduating from high school will have access to free career guidance, counseling and coaching from members who are already in their different professional career paths as they seek options that are particular to their interests.
Members are viable to volunteering opportunities where they will also be awarded with a certificate of merit by the end of their service. Besides certification, volunteers will have developed a wide range of knowledge, skills and experience for the service they provided during their volunteering period.
Members have access to referees and recommendation letters for all legal scholarships and jobs application.
Members will be able to develop a wide range of leadership skills; at the heart of the organization is the vision to produce leaders with high values of integrity from amongst all members through various activities, fellowships and trainings that they are engaged in within the organization.
