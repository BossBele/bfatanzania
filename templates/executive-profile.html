As a young visionary leader, Elisante Ephraim serves as the President of Builders Of Future Africa of which he began since September 2017 and formerly served as a President of the BHA(Children’s support foundation) in the year of 2016/2017.

He is also a CEO & Director General at Africamoja Youth Society a role which he assumed early January 2019. Before his appointment, Elisante was Director General leading all Africamoja activities in Tanzania.
As a prominent leader of the 21st Century, Elisante feels indebted to keep serving the community and thrive for prosperity and positive influence. Out of Africamoja, Elisante is pursued his Bachelor degree in Economics and statistics from the University of Dar Es Salaam. He currently mentors and provides financial consultation to Millennials who are passionate about Economic leadership and Entrepreneurship.
As an inspiring Public figure, Elisante holds exceptional skills in leadership and Project management where he holds significant academics and leadership recognition. He believes in capacity building, mentorship, hardworking, integrity and favored to work with millennials.
Elisante also sits as President of Dar es salaam University Economics Association (Economists’ support foundation) and works with Tukutane Porini safaris as a planning and operation officer.
